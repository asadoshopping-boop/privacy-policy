📸 SnapLock Quiz

Privacy Policy · Effective Date: March 24, 2026

Asado Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the SnapLock Quiz mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. All data is stored locally on your device.
  • Camera: Used solely to photograph study materials for quiz generation. Photos are processed on-device and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Gallery Access: Used to select existing images of study materials. Images remain on your device.
  • Study Notes & Quizzes: All notes, quizzes, and learning data are stored locally in the app's database on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • AI Quiz Generation: Extracted text (not images) from study materials is sent to an AI service to generate quiz questions. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests.
  • Google AdMob: Non-premium users may see advertisements. AdMob may collect device information as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing: Subscription purchases are handled by Google Play. Payment information is managed by Google, not by us.
  • Google ML Kit: Text recognition (OCR) is performed entirely on-device. No data is sent to Google for this purpose.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All user data (notes, quizzes, photos, statistics) is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not operate servers that store user data.
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is designed as an educational tool and does not require account creation or personal data input.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
